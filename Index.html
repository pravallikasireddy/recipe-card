Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serving Size: 4 people

Dry Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Pinch of Salt

Wet Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Essence
  • Vinegar / Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Mix and sieve all dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Mix sugar, oil, milk, vanilla, and lemon juice in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry mixes gently to make smooth batter.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 30–35 min or cook in preheated cooker on low flame for 40–45 min.
